Objective & Controls

Rescue loop: open compounds → land near hostages → open doors to load → return to base to unload.

  • Keyboard: Space fire, E doors (when grounded), Tab facing, R reverse.
  • Gamepad: left stick tilt, triggers lift, A doors, X fire, Y facing, B reverse.
